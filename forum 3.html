LOCATION OF INTI SUBANG

By Nurbekco-founder of EasyUni posted Jun 11, 2015

Just continuing my observation about INTI Subang from my yesterday's visit.

Location of INTI Subang is pretty good. It's about 5-10 min drive away from a huge shopping mall called Sunway Pyramid (which has all sorts of food outlets and entertainment) and KTM train station.

Outside the building there are a lot of food outlets. This one a big food court (Asian Cafe).











If you're into Starbucks, it's right in front of the INTI building too!
